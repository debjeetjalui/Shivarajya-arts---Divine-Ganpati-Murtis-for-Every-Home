About Shri Ganpati Murtis

Established in 1985, Shri Ganpati Murtis has been serving devotees with authentic, handcrafted Ganpati murtis for over three decades. Our workshop in Mumbai's traditional Kumharwada district continues the legacy of skilled artisans who pour their devotion into every creation.

We specialize in eco-friendly clay murtis that dissolve completely in water, maintaining the sanctity of our environment while honoring Lord Ganesha. Each piece is unique, carrying the essence of traditional Indian craftsmanship.
